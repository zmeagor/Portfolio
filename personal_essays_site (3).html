Zachary Meagor

About me: This is a collection of my writings and research. I am an undergraduate at the University of Southern California studying public policy on the pre-law track. I aim to go to law school and practice municipal law. Topics of interest include public policy, law, urban planning, philosophy, and art.

Public Policy

  • Public Art Master Plan- City of Morgan Hill

    During my development services internship at the City of Morgan Hill, I performed policy research and wrote numerous sections for the Public Art Master Plan. Adopted by the Morgan Hill City Council, the plan establishes a clear vision and framework for public art throughout the city, guiding future art installations and investments.
